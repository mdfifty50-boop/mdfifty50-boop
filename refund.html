Refund Policy

Effective Date: April 23, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 23, 2026

Summary: We offer a 14-day full refund for new subscribers. After 14 days, refunds are available for verified service outages. Micropayments via x402 are non-refundable once settled on-chain.

1. Subscription Refunds — 14-Day Guarantee

If you are a new subscriber and are not satisfied with our Services for any reason, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial subscription payment. No questions asked.

To request a refund within the 14-day window:

  • Email billing@mdfifty50-boop.github.io with your subscription email and the reason for the refund (optional)
  • We will process the refund within 5 business days
  • Refunds are returned to your original payment method

2. After the 14-Day Window

After the initial 14-day period, subscription payments are generally non-refundable. Exceptions include:

  • Service Outage: If our hosted services experience downtime exceeding 24 consecutive hours in a given billing month, you are entitled to a pro-rated credit for the affected period.
  • Billing Error: If you were charged incorrectly (wrong amount, duplicate charge, charge after cancellation), we will issue a full refund of the erroneous amount.
  • Accidental Renewal: If your subscription renewed automatically and you contact us within 7 days of the renewal date without having used premium features during that period, we may issue a refund at our discretion.

3. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard or by contacting us. Upon cancellation:

  • Your access to premium features continues until the end of the current billing period
  • No further charges will be made after cancellation
  • Cancellation does not automatically trigger a refund for the current billing period unless within the 14-day guarantee window

4. Micropayments (x402 / On-Chain)

Per-call tool payments made via the x402 protocol are settled as on-chain USDC transactions on the Base network. These transactions are non-refundable once confirmed on-chain, as they are irreversible by design.

If you were charged for a tool call that failed to deliver a result due to a server error on our end, contact us with the transaction hash and we will credit your account with an equivalent number of tool calls.

5. Open-Source Tools

Free, open-source versions of our MCP servers are provided at no cost under the MIT License. No payment is required and therefore no refund policy applies.

6. How to Request a Refund

Contact our billing team:

  • Email: billing@mdfifty50-boop.github.io
  • Include: your account email, subscription start date, and reason for refund request
  • Response time: within 2 business days
  • Processing time: 5–10 business days (depending on payment processor)

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated via email and posted on this page. The policy in effect at the time of your purchase applies to that transaction.

8. Contact

Questions about refunds? Reach us at billing@mdfifty50-boop.github.io
