Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 23, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 23, 2026

1. Overview

MCP Infrastructure ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our MCP server tools and related services. We collect minimal data and never sell your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create a paid account, we collect your name, email address, and payment method (processed by third-party payment processors — we never store raw card data).

Usage Data: For paid subscriptions, we may collect aggregated usage statistics (number of tool calls, error rates, uptime metrics) to improve our services. This data is anonymized and not linked to individual users beyond billing purposes.

Open-Source Tools: When you run our open-source MCP servers locally, no data is transmitted to us. The servers run entirely in your environment.

Agent Data Processed Locally: Our MCP servers process agent inputs and outputs locally in your environment. We do not receive, store, or have access to any agent conversations, tool inputs, or outputs from self-hosted deployments.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, subscription confirmations, service notices)
  • To provide customer support when you contact us
  • To improve our services using anonymized aggregate usage statistics
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Payment Processors: Paddle, Stripe (as applicable) — to process subscription payments. These processors have their own privacy policies and security standards.
  • Infrastructure Providers: GitHub (code hosting) — only code and public repository data.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request.

5. Data Storage and Security

Account and billing information is stored by our payment processor with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect any data we do store. Open-source tool data is stored entirely on your own infrastructure — we have no access to it.

6. SQLite Local Storage

Our MCP servers use SQLite databases stored locally in your deployment environment (default: ./data/ directory). These databases contain only data you generate through tool use. This data never leaves your environment. You control it entirely — you can delete the database files at any time.

7. Cookies

Our website uses only essential cookies required for authentication and security. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (right to erasure)
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability (receive your data in a structured format)

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@mdfifty50-boop.github.io. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Retention

We retain account and billing records for as long as your subscription is active and for up to 7 years afterward for tax and legal compliance purposes. Usage statistics are retained in anonymized form indefinitely. Upon account deletion, personal data is purged within 30 days except where retention is required by law.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Services are intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 16, we will delete it promptly.

11. International Transfers

Our infrastructure and payment processors may operate across multiple countries. By using our Services, you consent to the transfer of your data to jurisdictions that may have different privacy standards than your home country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any such transfers.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or prominent notice on our website. Continued use of the Services after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

For privacy questions, requests, or concerns:
Email: privacy@mdfifty50-boop.github.io
